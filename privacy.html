Privacy Policy

This game stores progress locally in your browser so you can continue on the same device.

  • Local saves: The game may store your current match and settings in localStorage.
  • No accounts: The game does not require a login or collect an account profile.
  • No ads or analytics: This build does not intentionally include ads, analytics, or third-party tracking scripts.
  • Hosting logs: If hosted publicly, the site host may collect standard server logs such as request times and IP addresses.
